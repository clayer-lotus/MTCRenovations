PROJECT FINANCING PROCESS AND FAQs

The financing for the home renovation project is provided through our financial partner, Financeit.

This is the process to be followed:

  • Please press here to access the Financeit application form. Please keep in mind that your credit application can only be considered if it is filed no later than 24 hours after the agreement is signed.
  • After you submit the application form, Financeit will send you an email letting you know whether or not the credit has been approved.
  • If accepted, you will see the loan's specifics, including the approved loan amount, the monthly payment, the loan's term, the amortization schedule, etc.
  • Only Financeit will be aware of the decision-making process if the credit is denied. If you have any questions about the justification for rejecting the loan application, feel free to contact them.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for MTC Renovations Home Renovation Loan

The loan granted is a HOME RENOVATION LOAN, and only the registered homeowner on the title of the property can apply for this loan. A Home Ownership Verification (HOV) task will need to be fulfilled by Financeit.

If you are not eligible for the full amount requested, a co-borrower may be added. This person does not have to be registered in the title of the property.

Proof of income (last pay slip or latest T4) is required by both the principal applicant and the co-borrower (if applicable).

The mentioned documentary evidence is to be uploaded within the Financeit application, which is the link that you receive via email once you have filled in the form for the loan application.
